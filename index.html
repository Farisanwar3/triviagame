Title: The Slimey Trivia High Concept: Take this trivia to create your own story about how you would deal with the lunch demon, slime, soggy sandwich, and the slug.

Player Experience Goals: Bring storytelling elements into a trivia and experience the story as you answer questions Once you play this game, you will become addicted to it.

Strategies: Just joke around when answering the questions There is no correct answer to every single question. Keep yourself relaxed as you answer more questions

Genre: Puzzle, Trivia

Game play: use buttons to select the answers

Target Audience: This game is for people who love funny things and literature in general For people over the age of 10.

Prototype: This game is inspired by the interview simulation games Instead of an interview, the game is a test with multiple choice questions. I wanted to do a minimalistic design that involved storytelling elements in a test game.

Setting/Premise: You take this test wherever and whenever you want

Story: There are many moral agents which look weird. You must decide what you think about them.

Prototype Assets: From Google images. Used ideas from brainstorming activity done last week. Used my imagination for many of the questions. Used many puns and made parodies of real life things.

Prototype Closest Other Game: One of the interview simulation games.
